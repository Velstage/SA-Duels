[VICTORIA DEFAULT SKIN - DETAILED DESIGN SCHEMA]

This schema defines Victoria’s default hero skin only.
It does not define her alternate outfits.

Default Skin Concept:
A sleek, dark, armored heroine suit for a mature veteran woman.
The design should feel elegant, intimidating, tactical, and commanding.
It should not feel cute, youthful, magical-girl-like, idol-like, or overly decorative.

Color Palette:
- primary: black
- secondary: charcoal gray / dark graphite
- metallic accent: muted silver
- optional subtle accent: very dark wine red or burgundy
- avoid bright colors
- avoid neon accents unless explicitly requested
- avoid colorful superhero toy-like styling

Mask:
- black physical eye mask
- covers the eye area and upper nose bridge
- angular but sleek
- frames the eyes sharply
- enhances her intimidating gaze
- must not look like eyebrows
- must not look like face paint
- must not expose too much forehead
- must not become oversized fantasy armor

Suit:
- dark armored bodysuit
- fitted but combat-ready
- sleek, structured, and refined
- strong shoulder line
- defined waist paneling
- armored chest and abdomen plates
- flexible dark combat fabric between armor sections
- not bulky
- not plain catsuit
- not lingerie-like
- not random fantasy armor

Chest Motif:
- sharp muted-silver V-shaped motif on the upper chest
- integrated into armor seams
- refined and minimal
- should emphasize her sharp heroic identity
- should not become a giant logo
- should not look like a corporate emblem
- should not glow unless specifically requested

Arms and Gloves:
- black armored gloves
- forearm guards integrated into the suit
- hands should usually be gloved in default combat skin
- armor should look sleek and practical
- avoid oversized gauntlets

Legs and Boots:
- fitted armored leg panels
- knee and shin protection
- dark combat boots
- boots should be sleek and practical
- avoid high stiletto heels
- avoid bulky military boots unless requested

Default Accessories:
- large silver hoop earrings
- clearly visible when ears are shown
- should look metallic and intentional
- do not replace with tiny studs
- do not replace with fantasy dangling jewels

Default Makeup:
- blue eyeshadow
- dark defined eye makeup
- deep wine or dark red lipstick
- mature glamorous makeup
- avoid cute pink makeup
- avoid innocent natural makeup
- avoid clown-like exaggeration

Default Skin Silhouette:
- strong shoulders
- defined waist
- balanced athletic hourglass figure
- tall, poised, mature presence
- suit should support Victoria’s powerful adult silhouette
- do not shrink her shoulders
- do not flatten her body into a plain tube shape
- do not exaggerate proportions into parody

Default Skin Negative Prompt:
Do not make the default skin cute, bright, childish, idol-like, magical-girl-like, lingerie-like, overly bulky, overly decorative, or generic. Do not turn the suit into a plain black catsuit. Do not remove the mask, silver hoop earrings, blue eyeshadow, dark lipstick, or the dark armored heroine identity.
